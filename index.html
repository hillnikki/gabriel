Transportation & Parking

St. Gabriel Parish is located right beside Bayview Village at Bayview & Sheppard. If you're driving, underground parking will be available. The church has entrances from Sheppard Avenue or from the streets on Elkhorn Drive.

Gardiner Museum

Gardiner Museum doesn't have their own parking lot, but Green P and street parking is available. You can get a look ahead on the Green P website as well.

Q&A

Q. When should I let you know whether or not I can make it?

You can officially let us know through our RSVP website here. If you want to let us know of anything informally, feel free to email us! We can be reached at: nikki.and.jeff.18@gmail.com.

Q. Can I invite someone to come with me?

If we assigned you a plus one, you can bring anybody you want to fit that spot. As long as you have open spot(s), you can bring anyone you want, but make sure to fill out their name and main course selection in the RSVP we sent out.

Q. What time should I be there for?

Check out the schedule for that! The ceremony starts at 2:00 PM, but try to be there at least 15 minutes earlier to be safe. The reception starts promptly at 6:00 PM, and we also recommend you get there at least 15 minutes ahead of time just in case.

Q. Is there a dress code?

Wear anything formal that you're comfortable in!

Q. Will there be parking at the ceremony and reception?

There will be both surface and underground parking at St. Gabriel's Parish, where our ceremony will be held.
As for the reception, there will be no on-site parking at Gardiner Museum, but there are Green P options to park downtown.

Q. How long will the event be?

The Ceremony will run from 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM. You'll have a short break between the ceremony and the reception, which will start with cocktails at 6:00 PM. Dinner will start at around 7:00pm and the party will run through to 11:00 PM. You can look at the schedule above for all of the events.

Q. Do you have any hotel rates we can use?

Yep, if you feel you want to stay closer to the reception, we have special rates with Strathcona Hotel. The rates are as follows:

  • Standard Room (King or Queen) $229 + applicable taxes.
  • Standard Room (Twin) $229 + applicable taxes.

To book a room, call (416) 363-3321 or 1-800-268-8304; or, email reservations@silverhotelgroup.com.
